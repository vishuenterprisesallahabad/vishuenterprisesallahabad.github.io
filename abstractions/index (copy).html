Abstractions

Hi, I’m Lakshya! I am a researcher at a deep-tech startup in India. I use this blog as a medium for writing about interesting ideas in ML/AI, quantum computing, and CS research in general. My aim through this blog is to get undergrad students in computing and information technologies excited about the field and motivate them to explore its depth and breadth. The core idea is to create a learning ecosystem in which research is viewed as a fundamental aspect of science-tech education.

This blog will be particularly useful to students that are interested in industry R&D or in graduate studies. Even if you're an absolute novice and need resources to build up, this blog is a nice place to start. Let's learn together!

To learn more about the philosophy of this blog, please read Why "I created abstractions?". If there is any way I could help you, please get in touch.

Computer Science

From ideas to algorithms to machines to languages, and from theory to systems to applications, computing is a phenomenal feat. I've tried to create a set of learning resources that students can refer to during their courses, beyond-the-course learning, and as the first steps into research. Simply put, it's a rigorous companion to your undergrad CS explorations that opens up a window to the vast expanse of computing and information.

Blogs, Papers, Books

I record my readings by writing explanatory research blogs, paper analyses and book reviews. While the papers are mostly related to my research interests, the books cover a vast expanse of computer science and related fields such as physics, maths, biology, neurosciene, philosophy, and business.
