About

Vishu Enterprises

We are the leading providers of enterprise-scale logistics and courier solutions in Allahabad with 19+ years of experience in executing pan-global shipments. We are one of the Channel Partners of DTDC and operate as their registered franchise.

We are 19+ years into the logistics business.

Vishu Enterprises was established in 2002 and is based in Allahabad, India.

  • We serve across the entire range of DTDC products and services.

    We provide a vast array of logistics solutions based on the needs of our clients.

  • We specialize in enterprise solutions and corporate partnerships.

    Our client base includes local businesses, offices, institutions, and hospitals.

  • We also support over-the-desk courier services for retail customers.

    Our store located in Tagore Town caters to the needs of individual customers.

Over the course of our journey, we have established a firm foothold in the logistics industry. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as our products and services have helped us a vast base of customers, and we aim for a larger client base.

Services

Check our Services

We offer all kinds of services including standard delivery, express delivery, premium logistics solutions, corporate partnerships, enterprise-scale freight and cargo. We provide a fast, reliable, and cost-effective solution based on client needs.

Standard & Express Delivery

We ensure a reliable delivery of consignements across 2200+ cities within the standard timelines.

Corporate Solutions

We partner with corporate clients, businesses and institutions for all their bulk logistics requirements.

Enterprise Solutions

We make special arrangements for enterprise customers including heavy consigenments and goods.

International Shipping

We provide services in 240+ international destinations for both inbound and outbound shipments.

01

Standard & Express Delivery

Standard services ensure a reliable delivery of documents and non-document parcels across 2200+ cities within the standard timelines. Express service sare expidated to deliver time-sensitve consignments.

02

Premium Services

Premium Services are designed for time-definite consignements and provide Next Business Day (NBD) assured delivery in metropoliton cities. Premium products include DTDC Plus, Blue, and Prime Time Plus.

03

Premium Express Cargo

Premium Express Cargo (PEC) is a guaranteed service for reliable and secure delivery of heavy consignments weighing between 25 to 100 Kgs. PEC Plus service ensures NBD delivery to select pin-codes.

04

International Services

We support inbound and outbound Express and Cargo services to 240+ international destinations. The modes of shipping include Door-to-Door, Door-to-Airport, and Airport-to-Airport Delivery based on client needs.

05

Supply Chain Solutions

DTDC has a highly optimized end-to-end supply chain service platform enhanced by advanced infrastructure, technology and a vast network. We provide Air Services, Surface Services, Rail Services, and Full Truck Load.

06

E-Commerce

DTDC's E-Commerce division (DotZot) supports order fulfillment, collection, transport, tracking and delivery of parcels. We also provide reverse logistics and drop-shipping services.
